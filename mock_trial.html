📖 How the Case Reader works

A quick tour — with examples built from your own case.
🗂️ Topic tabs (left side)

Each tab on the left is its own separate conversation for one part of your case. Switch between them anytime — each remembers its own chat.

  • 💬Ask Anything — general questions about the case.
  • 📢Opening Statement — themes, structure, and lines.
  • 👤Witnesses — direct exam prep, what to draw out.
  • 🔍Evidence — getting exhibits admitted, objections.
  • ⚠️My Weaknesses — find weak spots, track them with checkboxes.
  • ❓Cross-Exam — questions to attack the other side's witnesses.
  • 🔨Closing Argument — wrap-up structure and key points.
🔍 Deep Dive button

Normally the AI works from a short summary of your case (keeps it fast). Turn on 🔍 Deep Dive (next to the chat box) when you want it to read the exact wording of your script and tell you precisely what's wrong — it'll quote the real lines back to you.

✅ Checklists

On the Witnesses, Evidence, and Weaknesses tabs, a checklist shows up in the sidebar. Add things you need to handle and check them off as you go.
